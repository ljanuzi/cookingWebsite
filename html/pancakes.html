Pancakes

How to cook it

Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
White Sugar 1 tbsp
Baking Powder 2 tsp tsp
Salt 1 tsp
Egg, beaten 1
Vegetable Oil 2 tbsp

Method

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt
  2. Add the milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Cook untill is brown in both sides and serve hot.
