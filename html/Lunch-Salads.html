Honey Mustard Chicken Avocado and Bacon Salad

Ingredients Execution
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • 1/4 of an onion
  • 1 avocado
  • Half a cup of bacon bits

    • For the Dressing


  • Salt and Pepper
  • Half a lemon
  • 2 tablespoons of dijon mustard
  • 1 minced garlic
  • 4 boneless and skinless chicken thighs
  • 3 tablespoons of ground mustard
  • 1/3 of a cup[ honey
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  1. Dressing Prepartion:In a small bowl, add the olive oil, honey, stone ground mustard, Dijon mustard, lemon, garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  2. Remove half the marinade and refrigerate to use as a dressing.

  3. Add chicken thighs to remaining marinade and let it marinate for two hours.

  4. Add a teaspoon of olive oil to a nonstick pan. Sear chicken on each side until golden, crispy, and cooked through. Set aside and allow to rest.

  5. Put lettuce in a large bowl, add tomatoes, red onions, avocado slices and the bacon.

  6. Slice chicken into strips and place in the salad.

  7. Add the remaining marinade to the salad and toss around.

  8. Serve.

Caesar's Salad

Ingredients Execution
  • Croutons
  • Lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • 4 boneless and skinless chicken thighs

    • For the Dressing


  • Salt and Pepper
  • Half a lemon
  • 1 tablespoon of dijon mustard
  • 2 or 3 egg yolks
  • 1 small garlic clove
  • Half a cup of vegetable oil
  • 1/3 of a cup[ honey
  • Half a cup of olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons of finely grated Parmesan
  1. Dressing Prepartion:Chop together anchovy fillets, garlic, and pinch of salt. Use the side of a knife blade to mash into a paste, then scrape into a medium bowl. Whisk in egg yolks, 2 Tbsp. lemon juice, and mustard. Adding drop by drop to start, gradually whisk in olive oil, then vegetable oil; whisk until dressing is thick and glossy. Whisk in Parmesan. Season with salt, pepper, and more lemon juice, if desired.

  2. Add a teaspoon of olive oil to a nonstick pan. Sear chicken on each side until golden, crispy, and cooked through. Set aside and allow to rest.

  3. Put everything in a bowl and toss together.

  4. Serve.
