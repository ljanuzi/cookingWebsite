Bakewell tart - 1st baked dessert

A bakewell tart is an English dessert consisting of pastry shell beneath layers of jam, frangipane and and a topping of flammed almonds. Sometimes can be found as bakewell pudding, however theres quite a difference between these two.

Millefeuille with caramelized apples

The millefeuille iss a french dessert very similar to the Napoleon dessert but slightly different. It has three layers of pastry, two of cream and sometimes cocoa. The type of millefeuille with caramelized apples has ended up being the best within its type.
