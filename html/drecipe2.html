Spaghetti with prosciutto, garlic, parsley and fresh cream

- Pre-cooking -

Ingredients Quantity
Small red onion, finely chopped 1
Prosciutto, thinly sliced 4 ounces
Salt 4 tsp
Black pepper 4 tsp
Heavy cream Â½ cup
Spaghetti 12 ounces
Lemon(s) 1
Chopped Parsley 4 tbsp
Garlic Powder 2 tbsp

- Preperation -

For the steak:

1. Heat the olive oil. Add the onion and cook it until softened 6-8 minutes.
2. Add the prosciutto and cook it for 3-5 minutes
3. Add the garlic powder and black pepper
4. Put it all on a simmer, add the cream and keep stirring until is slightly thickened
5. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling water, and drain it well
6. Top the spaghetti with the prosciutto sauce
7. Add the parsley and serve immediately
Time to prepare 40min. Serves 4 people.
