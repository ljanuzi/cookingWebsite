Pan con Tomate with Garrotxa Cheese

Ingredients

  • Eight 1/2-inch-thick slices of ciabatta
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for brushing and drizzling
  • 2 garlic cloves, halved crosswise
  • 4 tomatoes, halved crosswise
  • Kosher salt
  • Pepper
  • Shaved Garrotxa cheese, for topping

Execution

Preheat the oven to 375Â°. Brush one side of each bread slice with oil and arrange oiled side up on a baking sheet. Bake for 10 minutes, until golden. Rub the toasts with the garlic cloves, then rub with the tomato halves until all of the flesh is gone; discard the tomato skins. Season the toasts with salt and pepper. Top with cheese, drizzle with olive oil and serve.
