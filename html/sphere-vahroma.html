Sphere valhrona chocolate with praline



TimeNew

Over all time required for this amazing dessert to be served is 1 hr and 15 minutes

Dishes needed New
  1. Flat tray
  2. Container
  3. Freezer
  4. Paco jet
  5. Sphere moulds
Yields

This dessert can be used for 10 servings



Ingredients

For praline jelly:
  • 4 sheets gold leaf gelatine
  • 500 ml boiron purée
For pliable chocolate:
  • 1 sheet gold leaf gelatine
  • 300 g valrohna milk chocolate
  • 350 g cream
  • 30 g water
  • 30 g water
  • 1 g salt
  • 3 g agar agar
Hazelnut Ice Cream:
  • 80 g sugar
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 150 ml full fat milk
  • 150 ml full fat cream
  • 50 g hazelnut valrohna praline
Chocolate soil:
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 100 g ground almonds
  • 60 g plain flour
  • 50 g cocoa powder
  • 70 g butter


Preperation

Praline jelly:

Soak gelatine sheets in iced cold water. Bring purée up to the boil and add in soaked gelatine and whisk until dissolved. Set in flat tray 2cm by 2cm put in fridge and set. Once set cut into 2cm cubes.

Pliable Chocolate:

Soak gelatine in iced water. Bring all ingredients up to boil add in gelatine and whisk until dissolved set in flat tray in fridge until needed.

Hazelnut Ice Cream :

Whisk sugar and egg yolks together until pale and doubled in size. Bring milk, cream and hazelnut praline up to boil then pour over egg and sugar mixture. Put into a container and set in freezer. Once set, put ice cream in paco jet and blend. Then put back in freezer until needed.

Chocolate Soil and shell:

Mix all ingredients together, spread on flat tray and dehydrate for one hour. Chocolate is tempered to 45 degrees and then cooled down to 26 degrees, then re heated to 30 degrees and poured into two semi sphere moulds to form the chocolate shell.Chocolate is tempered to 45 degrees and then cooled down to 26 degrees, then re heated to 30 degrees and poured into two semi sphere moulds to form the chocolate shell.
