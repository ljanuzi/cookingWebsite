Avocado Toast

Avocado toast is a kind of opened sandwich. It is healthy and it can be vegan or gluten free. It can be prepared very fast and effortlessly, so it is the perfect choice when you are running late in the morning.

How to cook it

Ingredients Quantity
Avocado 1/2
Lemon Juice 1/2 tbsp
Kosher Salt 1/8 tsp
Black Pepper 1/8 tsp
Grain Bread 1 oz
Red Pepper Flakes
Boiled and Sliced Egg 1

Method

  1. Mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt and black pepper using the back of a fork
  2. Top the toasted bread with mashed avocado mixture
  3. Drizzle with olive oil
  4. Add the egg slices
  5. Sprinkle the red pepper flakes
preperation serves
less than 10 minutes 1
