Risotto with gorgonzola

- Pre-cooking -

Ingredients Quantity
Arborio rice 1 cup
Chicken or vegetable broth 4 cups
Dry white wine ½ cup
Cold butter, divided 4 tbsp
Crumbled Dolce Gorgonzola 1 cup
Shallot, minced 1 large
Salt and pepper(s) Small amount, up to preference
Crunchy prosciutto 1 piece for decoration

- Preperation -

1. Bring the broth to a boil in a saucepan, over high heat. Once it's boiling, lower the heat to low to keep it warm.
2. In a large sauté pan, over high heat, melt the butter and add the shallots. Sauté the shallots until translucent.
3. Add the rice and stir, making sure the rice is coated with butter.
4. Add the wine, lower the heat to medium, and cook, stirring constantly, until it is almost completely evaporated.
5. Add 1 cup (or ladle) of broth and stir until the broth is almost completely absorbed.
6. Keep adding the broth, 1 cup at a time, and only adding the next cup once the broth is absorbed.
7. Once the rice is al dente (tender to the bite) but you still have some liquid, add the cold butter and the Gorgonzola. Stir vigorously (being careful not to break the rice grains) until the butter and cheese melt.
8. Put one slice of prosciutto on top and serve immediately.
