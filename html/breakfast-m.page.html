Breakfast

Breakfast is indeed the most important meal of the day. It is essential for everyone to eat breakfast every morning because it has so many benifits.Firstly it kick-starts your metabolism and helpes you burn calories throughout the day. Furthermore it gives you the energy you need for school or work.
Many studies link eating breakfast to good health, better memory and concentration. It lowers the chances of diabetes, heart disease and being overweight.
